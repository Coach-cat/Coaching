About Catherine

I believe every professional has a story worth telling. My role is to make that story stronger, sharper, and impossible for the right audience to miss.

With more than 25 years across coaching, professional writing, education, and career development, I bring a practical, highly personalized approach to helping people move forward with purpose. I work closely with each client to understand their experience, strengths, and goals — no templates, no one-size-fits-all advice.

My focus is helping clients understand what they offer, articulate it clearly, and present themselves more effectively across resumes, LinkedIn profiles, interviews, networking conversations, and career transitions. Editing and publishing a novel sharpened the writing craft I bring to every personal marketing piece — turning complex or overlooked experience into language that communicates value, impact, leadership, and transferable strength.

People often come to me when they feel stuck — unsure how to promote themselves or explain their background in a way that feels confident and authentic. Together we build clarity, momentum, and a clear message that reflects both who they are and where they're going.
